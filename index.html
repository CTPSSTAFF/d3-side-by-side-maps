Side-by-Side D3 MPO Town Maps


Data source: GeoJSON

Spatial data loaded from GeoJSON file. Derived from a clipped version of MassGIS TOWNSURVEY_POLYM layer.

  1. Maps are symbolized by FOURCOLOR attribute in the GeoJSON file, using 2 different color palettes.
  2. Includes simple D3 on-hover "title" tooltip for each town.
  3. Pan and zoom (from 0.2x to 5x) synchronized between the 2 maps.
